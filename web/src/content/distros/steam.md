**Valve's Arch-based gaming distribution** designed for the living room experience with
controller-friendly interface. Version 3.0 marks a complete rebuild on Arch with KDE Plasma,
offering desktop flexibility alongside gaming mode.

It powers the Steam Deck and brings console-like simplicity to PC gaming with Proton compatibility.
