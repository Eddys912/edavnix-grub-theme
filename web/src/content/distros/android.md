Google's **mobile operating system** based on a modified Linux kernel, designed primarily for
touchscreen devices. It dominates the global smartphone market with its customizable interface,
extensive app ecosystem through Google Play, and deep integration with Google services.

It's also found on tablets, smartwatches, and automotive systems.
