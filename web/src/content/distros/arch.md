A **rolling-release distribution** following the KISS principle with simplicity and user-centricity.
Users build their system from the ground up, installing only what they need. It features the Arch
User Repository (AUR), comprehensive documentation, and cutting-edge software.

Its DIY approach appeals to experienced Linux users seeking complete control.
