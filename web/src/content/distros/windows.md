**Microsoft's proprietary operating system** dominating the desktop market with its familiar GUI and
extensive software compatibility. While not Linux-based, it's often dual-booted or virtualized
alongside Linux. Its gaming support remains superior, though WSL (Windows Subsystem for Linux) now
offers integration with Linux distributions for developers.
