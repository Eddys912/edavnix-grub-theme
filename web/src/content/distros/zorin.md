An **Ubuntu-based distribution** specifically designed to ease Windows users' transition to Linux.
Its innovative layout switcher mimics Windows, macOS, or traditional Linux interfaces.

With built-in Wine integration for Windows applications and a user-friendly software store, it's
arguably the most accessible distribution for newcomers.
