A **visually stunning, user-friendly distribution** originally developed in China featuring the
elegant DDE desktop environment. It emphasizes aesthetic design with beautiful animations, a
macOS-like dock, and intuitive control panel. Despite concerns about Chinese telemetry, its beauty
and functionality have earned it a dedicated international following.
