One of the **most popular desktop distributions**, based on Ubuntu with the Cinnamon desktop environment. It follows a "just works" philosophy with multimedia codecs pre-installed and a familiar, traditional interface. Its update manager emphasizes safety and stability, making it an excellent choice for beginners and former Windows users seeking a hassle-free Linux experience.
